Fried Chicken

Description

Everyone's favorite food, every country has its own version of this dish! Here you can now make one for your self and your family!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • salt and peper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat
  2. Salt and pepper chicken pieces to taste, then roll in flour to coat
  3. Place chicken pieces in skillet and fry on medium heat until one side is golden brown
  4. Turn and brown other side until chicken is no longer pink inside and its juices run clear
  5. Drain on paper towel and serve!
